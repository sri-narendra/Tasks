Follow these steps exactly to link your GitHub Frontend with your Render Backend.

---
STEP 1: DEPLOY BACKEND (RENDER)
---
1. Go to your Render Dashboard (https://dashboard.render.com).
2. Click "New" > "Blueprint".
3. Connect your repository: https://github.com/sri-narendra/Tasks.git
4. Render will show a "Blueprint Config" page.
5. In the "Environment Variables" section, you MUST fill these:
   - MONGODB_URI: Your MongoDB Atlas connection string (e.g. mongodb+srv://...)
   - ALLOWED_ORIGINS: Enter `https://sri-narendra.github.io`
6. Click "Apply".
7. Wait for the build to finish. Once it says "Live", look at the top left of the Render page.
8. COPY THE URL (e.g., https://task-app-backend-xyz.onrender.com).

---
STEP 2: UPDATE FRONTEND CODE
---
1. In your local folder (or GitHub editor), open `src/config.js`.
2. Find the `BACKEND_URL` line.
3. Replace the placeholder with the REAL link you copied in Step 1:
   
   // --- src/config.js ---
   BACKEND_URL: isDev ? 'http://localhost:3000' : 'https://YOUR-RENDER-URL.onrender.com'
   
4. Save, commit, and push this change to GitHub.

---
STEP 3: ACTIVATE GITHUB PAGES
---
1. Go to your GitHub Repository > "Settings" > "Pages".
2. Under Build and deployment > Branch, select:
   - Branch: `main`
   - Folder: `/(root)`
3. Click "Save".
4. After 2 minutes, your site will be live at: https://sri-narendra.github.io/Tasks
